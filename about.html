About

We build Supersonic and ultra-fast drones in Switzerland to ensure they serve social good and to provide a safe, domestically produced alternative should malicious actors develop similar capabilities. From design through production we follow a value-sensitive approach and comply with the highest safety, ethical, legal, and manufacturing standards.

Our vision

The development of supersonic and generally extremely fast drones is inevitable: the current Guinness World Record, held by a German hobby developer, is 700 km/h.

It is therefore no longer a question of if such drones will be developed, but when and by whom. There is a clear necessity to develop these systems so they serve society in beneficial ways and—in worst-case scenarios—can be used to protect us.

Consequently, our primary purpose is to develop and deploy these drones for socially beneficial missions, since there are many situations where time is critical, for instance during disasters and other unpredictable events. Although we hope that the following scenario will never occur, secondarily our technology could also serve as a defensive option against malicious actors who may pursue similar endeavours with harmful intent.

We are proud to manufacture in Switzerland, adhering from the start to the highest safety, ethical, legal, and production standards through a value-sensitive design approach.
